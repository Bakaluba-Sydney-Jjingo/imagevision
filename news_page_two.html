Image Vision Limited marks 16 years of Existance

Image Vision Limited commemorated 16 years of existence with a special anniversary celebration.
The celebrations highlighted the past, present and future of the company.

“Held under the slogan “Deals with Quality Work Only.”, Image Vision Limited established, and has sustained its brand and legacy as the leading Photography and Videography company.”
Staff Reporter

Image Vision Limited established 25th June in 2003, has become one of the greatest globally trusted Ugandan iconic brands dedicated to serving and promoting in Uganda, East Africa and internationally.
