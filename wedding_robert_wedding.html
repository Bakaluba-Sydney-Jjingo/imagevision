ROBERT WEDS CATE

Share:

On 10 th / December / 2016, ROBERT wedded CATE at ST. PETERS' CATHEDRAL NSAMBYA.
It was a magnificent ceremony, where the bride and groom happily took Marriage committed vows and got a Marriage certificate inside the cathedral, on this day.

The church audiance was excited to hear vows being taken by their by couple and marriage committment encouragement given by priest while the church choir was harmonly singing with joy.

“We pride in trusting GOD to lead our journey of marriage.
He is able, Thank you GOD.”
Staff reporter

At the reception, the couple listened to words of wisdom from live speeches from their special invited guests representatives on how to start and stay on their marriage journey while the guests were joyfuly entertained by interesting jokes from the master of ceremony, the traditional dancing group and musicians while and after they were having their meals.
