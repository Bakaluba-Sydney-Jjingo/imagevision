SCRIPT WRITING AND CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT.

We plan by making research for writing meaningful and purposeful script and storyboard concept and technical instructions that suite client video production assignments.
Script formats may include dialogue, as well as decribing actions for the characters and what the camera sees.
We describe the scene, various shots to take and the most suitable location for any assignment as well as the charaters' dressing code.
We as well develop storyboards in the pre-production process to clearly convey how the story will flow, and what viewers will see and the best angle shots to take all together. By doing this we are able to see potential problems that would go unnoticed, ultimately saving the whole production time and money.
